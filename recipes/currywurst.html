Currywurst

Popular fast food item in Germany. Bratwurst with curry flavored ketchup. Eat with fries.

Ingredients

  • Brats, uncooked
  • Ketchup
  • Curry Powder

Steps

  1. Cook brats.
  2. While brats are cooking, mix ketchup with curry powder.
  3. Once brats are fully cooked, top with the curry ketchup.
  4. Garnish with curry powder.
