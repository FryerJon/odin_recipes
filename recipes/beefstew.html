General Tso's Chicken

Classic, feel good, comfort food. Chunks of beef stewed with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Beef, cubed
  • Vegetables, diced
  • Beef broth

Steps

  1. Brown the beef. No need to fully cook.
  2. Place browned beef in slow cooker.
  3. Add vegetables and beef broth to slow cooker.
  4. Place on high for 4 hours.
